Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Expender is committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, why, and how it is handled.

1. SMS Access

Expender requests permission to read your SMS inbox solely to parse M-Pesa transaction messages. This data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server, third party, or cloud service.

2. Data We Collect

  • Parsed transaction data — amounts, dates, recipient names, and transaction types extracted from M-Pesa messages.
  • Category rules — keywords you define yourself.
  • App preferences — settings and customisations stored locally.
  • User account — user account and subscriptions stored online and a local copy.

We do not collect your location, contacts, or any other information.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your device's private app storage. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data. We do not operate any analytics pipeline.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party — including advertisers, analytics providers, or data brokers.

5. Permissions Used

  • READ_SMS — to scan M-Pesa messages. Required for core functionality.

No other permissions are requested.

6. Children's Privacy

Expender is not directed at children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be noted with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@expender.app

Delete Your Account

Permanently remove your account and data

If you wish to delete your Expender account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Expender app on your Android device.
  2. Navigate to the Settings menu.
  3. Tap on "Account Card with your name and email address".
  4. Select the "Delete Account" option in RED.
  5. Review the confirmation message and tap "Delete" to proceed.
  6. Your account, including all transaction data and settings, will be permanently deleted.

Important: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be lost forever. If you have any subscriptions, they will be canceled automatically.
